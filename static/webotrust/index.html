GnuPG Web-of-trust

My friend Stefanus once maintained a little web-of-trust amongst our friends. He's stopped doing this now.

So I decided to do a talk on the GnuPG web-of-trust. I also wrote some software to support this. A Makefile is used to generate this graph, with the support of GnuPG, sig2dot, and Graphviz.

I've computed a web-of-trust amongst the people at the University of Waterloo, or at least, the people I know about. You can see a chart. But more interesting is the graph of our web-of-trust in PNG format. There's also a printable version in PostScript format.
